Support for Bockzee

Bockzee is a Yahtzee-style dice rolling and scorekeeper for tracking multi-player games with configurable players and roll limits.

1 to 6 players 3 to 5 rolls per turn Built-in scoring guide and rules

How Bockzee Works

  • Roll up to five dice and optionally hold dice between rolls.
  • Each turn allows a configurable number of rolls.
  • Score each turn into an open category on the score sheet.
  • When all categories are filled for all players, the highest total wins.
